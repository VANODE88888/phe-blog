Tranter Plate Heat Exchanger Model Guide

📅 Jul 2, 2026 ⏱ 6 min read

About Tranter

Tranter is a well-known American plate heat exchanger manufacturer, recognized for high cost-effectiveness and responsive service. Their product line includes the GX and GC series, widely used in HVAC, industrial cooling, and energy management.

Main Model Series

1. GX Series

GX7, GX8, GX12, GX18, GX26, GX37, GX42, GX51, GX51(clip), GX60, GX64, GX85, GX91, GX100, GX118, GX140, GX145, GX180, GX205, GX265

The GX Series is Tranter's main product line, featuring unique plate design with high heat transfer efficiency and easy maintenance. The GX60 is the most popular mid-size model.

2. GC Series

GC8, GC9, GC12, GC16, GC26, GC26(clip), GC30, GC51, GC51(clip), GC54, GC55, GC60, GC226

The GC Series offers compact design suitable for installations with limited space.

Tranter plate heat exchanger product photo

3. GL/GF Series

GL13, GL13(clip), GL230, GF97, GF187, 06T

The GL Series provides large-size heat exchangers, while the GF Series features free-flow channel design ideal for media containing particles.

Selection Recommendations

When selecting a Tranter plate heat exchanger, consider the following factors:

  • Flow Rate & Heat Load: Choose the appropriate model tier based on processing capacity and heat exchange requirements
  • Media Characteristics: Select corresponding plate materials (316L, titanium alloy, etc.) for corrosive media
  • Installation Space: Choose compact or standard models based on site constraints
  • Maintenance Convenience: Consider disassembly, cleaning frequency, and operational space

We recommend consulting Tranter or their authorized distributors before selection for the latest technical specifications and selection support.

Summary

Tranter offers a comprehensive range of plate heat exchanger models from micro to large industrial applications. The models listed in this article serve as a quick reference. Actual projects require detailed selection calculations based on specific operating conditions.
