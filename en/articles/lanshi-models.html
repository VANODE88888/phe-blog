Lanshi / LHE Plate Heat Exchanger Model Guide

📅 Jul 2, 2026 ⏱ 6 min read

About Lanshi / LHE

Lanshi (LHE) is a historic Chinese heavy equipment manufacturer. Their plate heat exchangers are known for high throughput, high pressure resistance, durability, and reliability. Leveraging strong heavy manufacturing capabilities, Lanshi has unique advantages in petrochemical and nuclear power fields.

Main Model Series

1. HT Series

HT101, HT102, HT103, HT104, HT108, HT150, HT151, HT152, HT153, HT156, HT201, HT206, HT231, HT232, HT233, HT234, HT352, HT502

The HT Series represents Lanshi's heavy-duty plate heat exchangers, designed for high temperature, high pressure, and high flow conditions. The H200/D is a commonly used industrial model.

Selection Recommendations

When selecting a Lanshi / LHE plate heat exchanger, consider the following factors:

  • Flow Rate & Heat Load: Choose the appropriate model tier based on processing capacity and heat exchange requirements
  • Media Characteristics: Select corresponding plate materials (316L, titanium alloy, etc.) for corrosive media
  • Installation Space: Choose compact or standard models based on site constraints
  • Maintenance Convenience: Consider disassembly, cleaning frequency, and operational space

We recommend consulting Lanshi / LHE or their authorized distributors before selection for the latest technical specifications and selection support.

Summary

Lanshi / LHE offers a comprehensive range of plate heat exchanger models from micro to large industrial applications. The models listed in this article serve as a quick reference. Actual projects require detailed selection calculations based on specific operating conditions. For spare parts procurement or model replacement, please provide the original nameplate information, and we will quickly match the corresponding product for you.
