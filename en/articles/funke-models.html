Funke Plate Heat Exchanger Model Guide

📅 Jul 2, 2026 ⏱ 6 min read

About Funke

Funke is a long-established German heat exchanger manufacturer, known for robust engineering and high durability. Their plate heat exchanger line covers applications from small HVAC to large industrial systems.

Main Model Series

1. FP Series

FP04, FP05, FP08, FP09, FP10, FP14, FP16, FP19, FP20, FP22, FP31, FP40, FP41, FP42, FP50, FP60, FP62, FP70, FP80, FP81, FP82, FP100, FP112, FP120, FP130, FP160, FP205, FP405

The FP Series is Funke's standard plate heat exchanger product line. The numbers indicate plate model and size. FP31 and FP70 are commonly used small-to-medium models, while FP205 and FP405 are large industrial-grade units.

Selection Recommendations

When selecting a Funke plate heat exchanger, consider the following factors:

  • Flow Rate & Heat Load: Choose the appropriate model tier based on processing capacity and heat exchange requirements
  • Media Characteristics: Select corresponding plate materials (316L, titanium alloy, etc.) for corrosive media
  • Installation Space: Choose compact or standard models based on site constraints
  • Maintenance Convenience: Consider disassembly, cleaning frequency, and operational space

We recommend consulting Funke or their authorized distributors before selection for the latest technical specifications and selection support.

Summary

Funke offers a comprehensive range of plate heat exchanger models from micro to large industrial applications. The models listed in this article serve as a quick reference. Actual projects require detailed selection calculations based on specific operating conditions. For spare parts procurement or model replacement, please provide the original nameplate information, and we will quickly match the corresponding product for you.
