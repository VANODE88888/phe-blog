Hisaka Plate Heat Exchanger Model Guide

📅 Jul 2, 2026 ⏱ 6 min read

About Hisaka

Hisaka is Japan's leading plate heat exchanger manufacturer, known for precision machining technology and exceptionally long service life. Hisaka products hold significant market share in Japan and Asia, especially in fine chemicals and electronics industries.

Main Model Series

1. LX Series

LX01, LX01A, LX10, LX10A, LX20, LX30, LX40, LX50

The LX Series represents Hisaka's standard plate heat exchangers for conventional liquid-to-liquid heat exchange applications.

Hisaka plate heat exchanger product photo

2. UX Series (Ultra-Compact)

UX01, UX05, UX10, UX20, UX30, UX40, UX80, UX90, UX100

The UX Series features ultra-compact design with minimal footprint and extremely high heat transfer efficiency — Hisaka's flagship product line. UX10 is the most popular model.

3. RX/SX/EX Series

RX10, RX30, RX70, SX40, SX90, EX11, EX15

The RX Series offers large-diameter industrial models, SX Series targets special operating conditions, and EX Series provides small compact designs.

Hisaka plate heat exchanger product photo

4. YX Series

YX80

YX Series designed for special applications.

Selection Recommendations

When selecting a Hisaka plate heat exchanger, consider the following factors:

  • Flow Rate & Heat Load: Choose the appropriate model tier based on processing capacity and heat exchange requirements
  • Media Characteristics: Select corresponding plate materials (316L, titanium alloy, etc.) for corrosive media
  • Installation Space: Choose compact or standard models based on site constraints
  • Maintenance Convenience: Consider disassembly, cleaning frequency, and operational space

We recommend consulting Hisaka or their authorized distributors before selection for the latest technical specifications and selection support.

Summary

Hisaka offers a comprehensive range of plate heat exchanger models from micro to large industrial applications. The models listed in this article serve as a quick reference. Actual projects require detailed selection calculations based on specific operating conditions. For spare parts procurement or model replacement, please provide the original nameplate information, and we will quickly match the corresponding product for you.
