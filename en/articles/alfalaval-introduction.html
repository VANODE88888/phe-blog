Alfa Laval: Global Leader in Plate Heat Exchanger Technology

📅 Jun 22, 2026 ⏱ 9 min read

Brand Overview

Alfa Laval, founded in 1883 and headquartered in Stockholm, Sweden, is a global leader in heat transfer, separation, and fluid handling technology. The company was named after its founder, Gustaf de Laval, who invented the centrifugal separator.

In the PHE field, Alfa Laval is one of the first companies to commercially produce plate heat exchangers. Their products are renowned for high efficiency, reliability, and advanced technology, widely used in HVAC, food & beverage, dairy, chemical, pharmaceutical, and marine industries.

Main Product Series

1. AlfaNova Series

  • Features: Fully welded plate structure, gasket-free design
  • Advantages: High temperature and pressure resistance, zero leakage risk, maintenance-free
  • Applications: Refrigerant systems, ammonia systems, high-temperature & high-pressure conditions
  • Typical models: AlfaNova 14, 27, 52, 76

2. T Series (T2, T5, T6, T8, T10, T20, T35, T45, T50, etc.)

  • Features: Classic gasketed PHE, most widely used
  • Advantages: Compact structure, high heat transfer efficiency, comprehensive model range
  • Applications: HVAC, industrial cooling, district heating, general industrial processes

3. M Series (M3, M6, M10, M15, M20, M30, etc.)

  • Features: Medium to large industrial PHE
  • Advantages: High throughput capacity, strong pressure resistance
  • Applications: Petrochemical, power generation, heavy industry

4. ClipLine Series

  • Features: Disassemblable PHE, easy maintenance
  • Advantages: Modular design, flexible plate count
  • Applications: Food processing, pharmaceutical, HVAC

5. FrontLine Series

  • Features: Hygienic design, smooth surfaces with no dead zones
  • Advantages: Easy CIP cleaning, meets food and pharmaceutical standards
  • Applications: Dairy, beverages, pharmaceutical

Technical Features

CurveFlow Distribution Area

Alfa Laval's patented flow distribution design ensures uniform fluid distribution across the entire plate surface, avoiding dead zones and flow short-circuiting, improving heat transfer efficiency and reducing fouling.

OmegaPort Corner Ports

Elliptical corner port design provides more uniform fluid distribution compared to traditional round ports, reducing pressure drop.

FlexFlow Plate Geometry

Some models support asymmetric plate design, allowing different corrugation angles on hot and cold sides, balancing heat transfer efficiency and pressure drop requirements.

Five Corrugation Angles

Alfa Laval offers plates with various corrugation angles (30°, 45°, 60°, 90°), allowing flexible selection based on heat transfer and pressure drop needs.

Common Model Cross-Reference

If you see model numbers on equipment nameplates such as:

  • M6-MFG, M10-BFG, M15-BFG
  • T5-MFG, T20-MFG
  • AlfaNova 52

These are standard Alfa Laval models. Note that different models correspond to different plate sizes, connection sizes, and pressure ratings. When replacing spare parts, always verify the exact model and serial number.

Maintenance & Spare Parts

Key points for Alfa Laval PHE maintenance:

  1. Regular gasket inspection — check for aging, cracking, or deformation
  2. Monitor pressure drop changes — abnormal increase may indicate fouling or blockage
  3. Clean plates — chemical cleaning or high-pressure water flushing
  4. Correct tightening — tighten bolts to the manufacturer's specified A-dimension (compression dimension)
  5. Use OEM spare parts — ensure gasket and plate compatibility and sealing reliability

Summary

As the global technology leader in PHE, Alfa Laval offers a complete product line from disassemblable gasketed exchangers to fully welded units, from small HVAC applications to large industrial projects. If your project requires efficient, reliable, and well-known heat exchange solutions, Alfa Laval is typically one of the top choices.
