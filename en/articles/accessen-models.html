Accessen Plate Heat Exchanger Model Guide

📅 Jul 2, 2026 ⏱ 6 min read

About Accessen

Accessen is China's leading plate heat exchanger manufacturer, headquartered in Shanghai. With high-quality domestic production and responsive service advantages, Accessen has extensive applications in domestic HVAC, power, and chemical industries.

Main Model Series

1. AU/AN Standard Series

AU3, AU5, AU8, AU20, AN5, AN10M, AN20

The AU Series offers small compact designs while the AN Series provides medium-sized high-efficiency units for conventional liquid-to-liquid heat exchange.

Accessen plate heat exchanger product photo

2. AN-L Large Port Series

AN10L1, AN10L2, AN15L1, AN15L2, AN25L1, AN25L2, AN30L1, AN30L2, AN30L3, AN35L1, AN35L2, AN35L3, AN35L4, AN40L1, AN40L2, AN40L3, AN40L4, AN45L1, AN45L2, AN45L3, AN45L4

The AN-L Series features enlarged port design for high-flow, low-pressure-drop conditions. AN40L2 is a commonly used large industrial model.

3. AU-L Series

AU10L1, AU10L2, AU15L1, AU15L2, AU15M, AU20M

The AU-L Series combines the compactness of the AU Series with large port design.

Accessen plate heat exchanger product photo

4. AS Series

AS20, AS25

The AS Series is designed for special operating conditions such as high-viscosity media or extreme temperature conditions.

Selection Recommendations

When selecting a Accessen plate heat exchanger, consider the following factors:

  • Flow Rate & Heat Load: Choose the appropriate model tier based on processing capacity and heat exchange requirements
  • Media Characteristics: Select corresponding plate materials (316L, titanium alloy, etc.) for corrosive media
  • Installation Space: Choose compact or standard models based on site constraints
  • Maintenance Convenience: Consider disassembly, cleaning frequency, and operational space

We recommend consulting Accessen or their authorized distributors before selection for the latest technical specifications and selection support.

Summary

Accessen offers a comprehensive range of plate heat exchanger models from micro to large industrial applications. The models listed in this article serve as a quick reference. Actual projects require detailed selection calculations based on specific operating conditions. For spare parts procurement or model replacement, please provide the original nameplate information, and we will quickly match the corresponding product for you.
