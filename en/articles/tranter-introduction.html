Tranter: An American Powerhouse in Plate Heat Exchangers

📅 Jun 1, 2026 ⏱ 8 min read

Brand Overview

Tranter was founded in 1932 and is headquartered in Texas, USA. As a globally recognized PHE manufacturer, Tranter has a long development history and extensive engineering application experience. Their products are known for being robust, durable, efficient, and cost-effective.

Tranter has multiple manufacturing facilities and sales/service networks worldwide, widely serving petrochemical, power generation, HVAC, food & pharmaceutical, and marine/heavy industries. In the Chinese market, Tranter enjoys strong brand recognition and market share.

Main Product Series

1. GX Series Gasketed PHE

  • Models: GX-005, GX-012, GX-026, GX-042, GX-051, GX-060, GX-100, GX-145, GX-205, etc.
  • Features: Standard disassemblable design, comprehensive model range
  • Advantages: Flexible application, easy maintenance
  • Applications: HVAC, industrial cooling, district heating, general industrial processes

2. GC Series

  • Models: GC-008, GC-016, GC-026, GC-030, GC-052, GC-060, GC-100, etc.
  • Features: Compact design
  • Applications: Small-to-medium heat exchange applications

3. GF Series

  • Models: GF-057, GF-097, GF-138, GF-257, etc.
  • Features: Free-flow channel design, larger plate gap
  • Applications: Media containing particles, fibers, or viscous fluids

4. GXD/GCD Series (Double-Wall)

  • Features: Double-wall plate design to prevent cross-contamination
  • Applications: Food & beverage, pharmaceutical, nuclear, and other high-safety applications

5. Superchanger Semi-Welded

  • Features: Semi-welded construction, suitable for refrigerant applications
  • Applications: Ammonia systems, refrigeration, air conditioning

6. Maxchanger Welded

  • Features: Fully welded construction, gasket-free
  • Applications: High temperature, high pressure, corrosive media

Technical Features

Multiple Corrugation Angles

Tranter plates offer multiple corrugation angle options, allowing optimized combinations based on heat transfer and pressure drop requirements:

  • Low-angle corrugation: Low pressure drop, suitable for viscous media
  • High-angle corrugation: High heat transfer coefficient, slightly higher pressure drop

Loc-in Gasket System

Tranter uses a unique gasket fixing method where gaskets snap firmly onto plates, preventing detachment during disassembly and improving maintenance efficiency.

Plate Material Options

  • 304/316 Stainless Steel: General water and oil media
  • Titanium: Seawater, chloride-containing media
  • Hastelloy: Highly corrosive media
  • SMO 254: High-chloride environments

Tranter Spare Parts & Services

Tranter has service centers in China providing:

  • PHE sizing calculations

  • On-site cleaning and maintenance
  • Equipment modification and capacity expansion

Common Spare Parts

  • Rubber gaskets (NBR, EPDM, Viton, etc.)
  • Stainless steel plates

  • Tightening bolts and nuts
  • Fixed/movable cover plates
  • Guide bars

Summary

Tranter represents American PHE manufacturing excellence, with a product line covering gasketed, semi-welded, fully-welded, and brazed types, offering a comprehensive model range and wide application scope. With robust, durable design and a complete global service network, Tranter heat exchangers enjoy a strong reputation in the Chinese market and are a reliable choice for industrial heat exchange applications.
