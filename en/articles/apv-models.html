APV Plate Heat Exchanger Model Guide

📅 Jul 2, 2026 ⏱ 6 min read

About APV

APV is a core brand under SPX FLOW, with over a century of heat exchange technology expertise. APV plate heat exchangers are known for excellent sealing technology and high reliability, widely used in food & beverage, pharmaceutical, and dairy industries.

Main Model Series

1. A/H Series

A055, A085, A145, H12, H17, H30

The A/H Series represents APV's classic small-to-medium heat exchangers with unique plate corrugation design delivering high heat transfer efficiency. The H17 is one of the most commonly used models in this series.

APV plate heat exchanger product photo

2. B Series

B063, B110M, B110S, B134M, B134S, B158M, B158S, B205S

The B Series comprises medium-to-large frame heat exchangers suitable for moderate flow applications.

3. J/K/N Series

Junior, J060, J092, J107, J185, K34, K55, K71, N25, N35, N50

The J Series features compact design, the K Series offers reinforced construction, and the N Series represents new high-efficiency designs.

4. Q/R/SR Series

Q030E, Q030D, Q055E, Q055D, Q080E, Q080D, R5, R6, R106, R405, R8GI, SR1, SR2, SR3, SR6GH, SR6GL, SR6AG, SR6AA, SR9, SR14GD, SR14AP, SR14AD, SR14AH, SR23PV

The Q/R/SR Series offers professional-grade heat exchangers with various plate sizes and corrugation patterns.

5. Others

LR9GN, LR9GL, LR9AV, M66, M92, M107, P105, P190, C063, C205, TR9AV, TR9GN, TR9GL, TR9AL, T4, 0050, 0034, 2155

Selection Recommendations

When selecting a APV plate heat exchanger, consider the following factors:

  • Flow Rate & Heat Load: Choose the appropriate model tier based on processing capacity and heat exchange requirements
  • Media Characteristics: Select corresponding plate materials (316L, titanium alloy, etc.) for corrosive media
  • Installation Space: Choose compact or standard models based on site constraints
  • Maintenance Convenience: Consider disassembly, cleaning frequency, and operational space

We recommend consulting APV or their authorized distributors before selection for the latest technical specifications and selection support.

Summary

APV offers a comprehensive range of plate heat exchanger models from micro to large industrial applications. The models listed in this article serve as a quick reference. Actual projects require detailed selection calculations based on specific operating conditions. For spare parts procurement or model replacement, please provide the original nameplate information, and we will quickly match the corresponding product for you.
