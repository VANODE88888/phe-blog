Alfa Laval Plate Heat Exchanger Model Guide

📅 Jul 2, 2026 ⏱ 6 min read

About Alfa Laval

Alfa Laval is the global technology leader in plate heat exchangers, with a product line spanning from micro to large industrial applications. Their PHEs are renowned for high heat transfer efficiency, compact design, and long service life.

Main Model Series

1. M Series (Standard)

M3, M3X, M6, M6M, M6MX, M6MW, M10, M10BW, M10M, M10MD, M15M, M15F, M20MW, M30

The M Series is Alfa Laval's most versatile product line, suitable for most standard heat exchange conditions with herringbone corrugation plate design.

Alfa Laval plate heat exchanger product photo

2. TL Series (Brazed)

TL3P, TL3B, TL6B, TL10B, TL10P, TL15B, TL35B, TL350S

The TL Series features fully welded brazed plate heat exchangers with compact structure and high pressure resistance, commonly used in refrigeration, heat pumps, and hydraulic oil cooling.

3. T Series (Semi-Welded)

T2B, T5B, T5M, T8B, T8M, T20B, T20BW, T20M, T20P, T2OS, T35P, T45M, T50M

The T Series uses laser semi-welding technology with welded seal on one side and gasket seal on the other, ideal for refrigerants and other special media.

4. A Series (Industrial)

A10B, A15B, A20B, A30, A35, AM10, AM20, AM20B, AM2ON, AX30B, AK20, AQ80

The A Series comprises large frame-type plate heat exchangers for major industrial applications such as petrochemical, power generation, and marine.

5. P Series / VAP / EC / CLIP

P2, P16, P22, P26, P30, P31, P32, P36, VAP350, VAP500, EC350, EC500, CLIP3, CLIP6, CLIP8, CILP10

The P Series is designed for heavy industries like power plants and petrochemicals with high temperature and pressure resistance. VAP series serves as evaporators for steam heating. EC and CLIP series offer economical compact design.

Alfa Laval plate heat exchanger product photo

6. Others (AC/MX/MA/MK)

AC400, AC600, MX25B, MX25M, MA30M, MA30S, MA30W, MK15BW

Selection Recommendations

When selecting a Alfa Laval plate heat exchanger, consider the following factors:

  • Flow Rate & Heat Load: Choose the appropriate model tier based on processing capacity and heat exchange requirements
  • Media Characteristics: Select corresponding plate materials (316L, titanium alloy, etc.) for corrosive media
  • Installation Space: Choose compact or standard models based on site constraints
  • Maintenance Convenience: Consider disassembly, cleaning frequency, and operational space

We recommend consulting Alfa Laval or their authorized distributors before selection for the latest technical specifications and selection support.

Summary

Alfa Laval offers a comprehensive range of plate heat exchanger models from micro to large industrial applications. The models listed in this article serve as a quick reference. Actual projects require detailed selection calculations based on specific operating conditions.
