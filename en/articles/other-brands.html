Overview of Other Major Plate Heat Exchanger Brands

📅 May 25, 2026 ⏱ 11 min read

Japanese Brand: Hisaka

Hisaka is a renowned Japanese PHE manufacturer, founded in 1941. Hisaka products are known for precision manufacturing and consistent quality, widely used in Asian markets, particularly Japan, China, and Southeast Asia.

Key Features

  • High-precision plate processing
  • Long gasket service life
  • Product series covering HVAC, industrial, and food applications
  • Rich experience in marine, nuclear, and other special applications

Common Models

  • UX Series: UX-01, UX-10, UX-20, UX-40, UX-80, UX-115, UX-195, etc.
  • LX Series, SX Series, RX Series, etc.

Danfoss / Sondex

Sondex, originally a Danish brand, was later acquired by Danfoss. Sondex PHEs are known for cost-effectiveness and a comprehensive model range.

Main Series

  • S Series: S4, S7, S8, S9, S14, S19, S20, S21, S22, S37, S41, S43, etc.
  • SW Series: Semi-welded
  • SF Series: Free-flow

Vicarb

Vicarb is a French brand, now part of the Alfa Laval group. Vicarb heat exchangers have strong recognition in the European market, known for robust, durable construction.

Main Models

  • V2, V4, V8, V13, V20, V28, V45, V60, V100, V120, V130, etc.

Accessen

Accessen is a leading Chinese PHE manufacturer, founded in 2002. Accessen has introduced advanced foreign technology, holding an important position in the domestic market and gradually expanding internationally.

Key Features

  • Cost-effective
  • Complete product line
  • Fast localized service response
  • Customizable to customer requirements

Common Models

  • AU Series, AX Series, AC Series, etc.

SWEP

SWEP is a Swedish brand focused on brazed plate heat exchangers (BPHE) and is one of the world's leading brazed heat exchanger manufacturers.

Main Applications

  • Air conditioning and heat pump systems
  • Refrigeration equipment
  • Domestic hot water systems
  • Oil coolers

Common Models

  • B Series, E Series, P Series, V Series, etc.

Funke

Funke is a renowned German heat exchanger manufacturer, founded in 1974. Funke products are known for German quality and include plate heat exchangers, shell-and-tube exchangers, and air-cooled exchangers.

Features

  • Rich product variety
  • High degree of customization
  • Widely used in oil, chemical, and mechanical engineering

Common Models

  • FP Series: FP04, FP08, FP14, FP16, FP20, FP22, FP31, FP40, FP50, FP60, FP70, FP80, FP100, etc.

Lanshi

Lanshi (Lanzhou Lanshi Heat Exchange Equipment Co., Ltd.) is a veteran Chinese heat exchanger manufacturer, part of the Lanshi Group. Lanshi is an important participant in China's PHE industry, with products widely used in petroleum, chemical, power, and metallurgy sectors.

Features

  • State-owned enterprise background, strong technical capabilities
  • Strong large heat exchanger manufacturing capacity
  • Widely applied in energy and chemical sectors

Common Models

  • H Series, B Series, and multiple other models

Brand Selection Guide

Requirement Recommended Direction
Premium quality, global service Alfa Laval, GEA, APV
Cost-effectiveness, fast delivery Tranter, Accessen, domestic manufacturers
Brazed heat exchangers SWEP, Alfa Laval
Special materials/corrosive media Alfa Laval, GEA, Hisaka
Large industrial projects GEA, Lanshi, Alfa Laval
Food/pharma hygienic grade APV, Alfa Laval, GEA

Summary

The PHE market is fully competitive, featuring international tier-one brands like Alfa Laval, GEA, and APV; distinctive brands like Tranter, Hisaka, Sondex, Vicarb, and Funke; and excellent domestic manufacturers like Accessen and Lanshi. Brand selection should comprehensively consider technical requirements, budget, delivery timeline, after-sales service, and other factors to find the best solution for your project's actual needs.
