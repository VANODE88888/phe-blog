Sondex / Danfoss Plate Heat Exchanger Model Guide

📅 Jul 2, 2026 ⏱ 6 min read

About Sondex / Danfoss

Sondex, now part of the Danfoss Group, is a well-established European plate heat exchanger manufacturer. Their products excel in cost-effectiveness and flexible customization, with a broad customer base across Europe and global markets.

Main Model Series

1. S Series (Standard)

S4A, S7A, S8A, S9A, S14A, S15, S17, S18, S19, S19A, S20, S20A, S21, S21A, S22, S25, S30, S31A, S35, S36, S37, S38, S38A, S39, S41, S41A, S42, S43, S43(glued), S43B, S47, S47A, S47B, S50, S58, S62, S62B, S64, S65, S65B, S65(glued), S67, S81, S86, S86B

The S Series is Sondex's most extensive product line, covering virtually all flow ranges from the micro S4A to the large S86. S121 and S188 are the most popular medium-to-large models.

Sondex / Danfoss plate heat exchanger product photo

2. S Large Series

S100, S100(glued), S100A, S110, S110B, S121, S130, S136, S145, S152, S155, S187, S188, S201

Models above S100 are large industrial heat exchangers for high-flow, high-heat-load applications.

3. SF/SW Series

SF11, SF25A, SF53, SF123, SF160, SFD13, SFD22, SFD23, SW40, SW229

The SF Series features free-flow channel design for media containing fibers or particles. The SW Series offers wide-gap design.

Sondex / Danfoss plate heat exchanger product photo

4. Danfoss XG Series

XG30, XG40, XG45, XG50, XG65, XG75, XG85, XG95, XGS55A, XGS56A

The Danfoss XG Series represents Danfoss's own brand of brazed and semi-welded plate heat exchangers.

Selection Recommendations

When selecting a Sondex / Danfoss plate heat exchanger, consider the following factors:

  • Flow Rate & Heat Load: Choose the appropriate model tier based on processing capacity and heat exchange requirements
  • Media Characteristics: Select corresponding plate materials (316L, titanium alloy, etc.) for corrosive media
  • Installation Space: Choose compact or standard models based on site constraints
  • Maintenance Convenience: Consider disassembly, cleaning frequency, and operational space

We recommend consulting Sondex / Danfoss or their authorized distributors before selection for the latest technical specifications and selection support.

Summary

Sondex / Danfoss offers a comprehensive range of plate heat exchanger models from micro to large industrial applications. The models listed in this article serve as a quick reference. Actual projects require detailed selection calculations based on specific operating conditions. For spare parts procurement or model replacement, please provide the original nameplate information, and we will quickly match the corresponding product for you.
