Thermowave Plate Heat Exchanger Model Guide

📅 Jul 2, 2026 ⏱ 6 min read

About Thermowave

Thermowave is a well-known German manufacturer of brazed plate heat exchangers, renowned for high-quality stainless steel brazing technology. Their products are widely used in refrigeration, air conditioning, heat pumps, and industrial cooling.

Main Model Series

1. TL Brazed Series

TL50SS, TL50PP, TL90SS, TL90PP, TL150SS, TL150PP, TL250SS, TL250PP, TL400SS, TL400PP, TL500SS, TL500PP, TL650SS, TL650PP, TL850SS, TL850PP

The TL Series features all-stainless-steel brazed plate heat exchangers. SS designates Single System, PP designates Parallel Plate. The TL250 is the most commonly used mid-size model.

Selection Recommendations

When selecting a Thermowave plate heat exchanger, consider the following factors:

  • Flow Rate & Heat Load: Choose the appropriate model tier based on processing capacity and heat exchange requirements
  • Media Characteristics: Select corresponding plate materials (316L, titanium alloy, etc.) for corrosive media
  • Installation Space: Choose compact or standard models based on site constraints
  • Maintenance Convenience: Consider disassembly, cleaning frequency, and operational space

We recommend consulting Thermowave or their authorized distributors before selection for the latest technical specifications and selection support.

Summary

Thermowave offers a comprehensive range of plate heat exchanger models from micro to large industrial applications. The models listed in this article serve as a quick reference. Actual projects require detailed selection calculations based on specific operating conditions. For spare parts procurement or model replacement, please provide the original nameplate information, and we will quickly match the corresponding product for you.
